# The Truth About Free Backend Options

Here's the reality: ALL free serverless platforms (Vercel, Netlify, etc.) reset memory between requests. 

For truly persistent data, you need one of these:

## Option 1: Railway.app (Simplest)
- Sign up at https://railway.app
- Deploy your simple-backend folder
- You get $5 free credit monthly (enough for metrics)
- Just drag and drop!

## Option 2: Render.com 
- Sign up at https://render.com
- Deploy the simple-backend as a "Web Service"
- Free tier available
- Sleeps after 15 min but wakes up when accessed

## Option 3: Just Accept It
- Your app works PERFECTLY
- Sync feature is there for future use
- Metrics showing 0 doesn't break anything
- You can add real persistence later

## Option 4: Use Your Phone/Computer
- Run the backend on an old phone/computer at home
- Use ngrok to expose it to internet
- Free forever but needs device to stay on
