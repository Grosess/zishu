# Privacy Policy for Zishu - Hanzi Writing Practice

**Last Updated: June 17, 2025**

## Overview

Zishu ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our offline Chinese character writing practice application handles your information.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Collect
The App collects and stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

1. **Practice Progress Data**
   - Characters practiced
   - Practice session timestamps
   - Accuracy statistics
   - Custom character sets you create

2. **User Preferences**
   - Selected character sets
   - Display settings
   - Practice mode preferences

### What We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Device identifiers
- Analytics or tracking data
- Any data transmitted over the internet

## How Your Data is Used

All data is used exclusively to:
- Track your learning progress
- Provide personalized practice sessions
- Save your custom character sets
- Maintain your app preferences

## Data Storage

- **100% Offline**: All data is stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not use any cloud services
- **No External Servers**: The app does not connect to any external servers
- **No Third-Party Services**: We do not share data with any third parties

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- Device-level security measures (PIN, password, biometric authentication)
- Standard iOS/Android app sandboxing
- No network transmission of personal data

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:
1. Uninstalling the app from your device
2. Using the "Clear All Data" option in the app settings
3. Clearing app data through your device's settings

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages as it does not collect or transmit personal information.

## Third-Party Content

The App includes Chinese character stroke data from:
- MakeMeAHanzi project (stroke order data)
- CC-CEDICT (character definitions)
- Unicode Unihan Database (character information)

These resources are included in the app and do not require internet connectivity.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please create an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the app store where you downloaded Zishu.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:
- View all stored data within the app
- Export your progress data
- Delete any or all data at any time
- Use the app without providing any personal information

By using Zishu, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy.
