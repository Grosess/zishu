## Full Description

**Learn to write Chinese characters the right way with Zishu!**

Zishu is your personal Chinese character writing tutor that works completely offline. Practice writing Hanzi with accurate stroke order validation, visual feedback, and progress tracking - all without an internet connection.

**Key Features:**

📝 **Authentic Stroke Order**
- Real stroke data from the MakeMeAHanzi project
- Validates each stroke as you write
- Visual hints after wrong attempts

✨ **Smart Practice System**
- Characters organized by difficulty and frequency
- HSK level sets (1-6) included
- Create custom character sets
- Track your progress over time

🎯 **Immediate Feedback**
- Green checkmark for correct strokes
- Red X for incorrect attempts
- Animated hints show proper technique
- Build muscle memory through repetition

📊 **Progress Tracking**
- See your accuracy statistics
- Track practice streaks
- Monitor improvement over time
- Export your progress data

🔒 **100% Offline & Private**
- No internet required
- All data stays on your device
- No ads or tracking
- No account needed

📚 **Comprehensive Content**
- Thousands of characters included
- Pinyin and English definitions
- Traditional and simplified characters
- From basic to advanced levels

**Perfect for:**
- Students learning Chinese
- Teachers needing practice tools
- Anyone interested in Chinese culture
- Parents helping children learn

**Why Zishu?**
Unlike other apps that just show you characters, Zishu actively teaches you proper stroke order through practice. Our validation system ensures you're learning to write characters correctly from the start.

Start your journey to Chinese character mastery today with Zishu!
